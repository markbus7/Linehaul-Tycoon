🎯 Cargo focus

🏆 Goals

How to play

The goal

Build a freight empire across Europe. Contracts appear at your hubs — move the cargo, get paid, level up, grow your network and fleet.

Contracts

Each contract is a load going from one hub to another, showing cargo type, weight (t) and payout. Tap Dispatch and your nearest suitable idle vehicle drives to pick it up, then delivers. You're paid on delivery.

Capacity & cargo

A vehicle can take a contract only if its capacity ≥ the load's weight. Bigger vehicles carry heavier, higher-paying loads. Refrigerated 🧊 loads need a Reefer vehicle.

Loading runs (the real game)

Tap an idle vehicle → Load / move. Fill it with several contracts from its hub (even to different cities) and it runs an efficient multi-stop delivery, paying on each drop. The load screen shows gross, fuel and net so you can judge if a run is worth it.

Every choice is a trade-off

Bigger capacity = more profit per run — but the vehicle is slower, burns more fuel, and takes longer to load (load time grows with the tonnes aboard). Small / express vehicles carry less but cycle faster and sip fuel — best for short, frequent, high-value loads. Fuel is charged per leg (distance × how full you are); even empty repositioning burns some. So the Loading bays and Fuel cards upgrades really pay off once you run big rigs.

Hub tiers & vehicle class

Hubs come in three sizes — 🟠 major, 🔵 regional, ⚪ feeder. Big vehicles can only serve larger hubs; small vans serve everywhere. So you build a mix: vans feeding small towns, big rigs running the trunk lanes between major hubs.

Four ways to move freight

🚚 Road — flexible, reaches every hub, low–mid capacity. 🚂 Rail — cheap per-tonne overland bulk, fast-ish, but only between rail hubs on the same continent. 🚢 Sea — massive, cheapest per tonne, very slow, ports only, crosses oceans. ✈️ Air — fast and ocean-crossing, low capacity, pricey fuel.

The whole world 🌍

Six continents: Europe, the Americas, Africa, Asia and Oceania. Each is its own landmass — road and rail stay within a continent; to cross any ocean you need a plane or ship. Loads that must go sea/air show a 🌊 tag. The map opens on your hubs and zooms out as you expand — unlock outward from the Benelux and build a global network.

Auto-dispatch 🤖

When ON, every idle vehicle automatically grabs the best contract it can carry, so you don't tap Dispatch yourself. Unlock it in Upgrades (Level 3), then toggle it in the top bar. Great once your fleet is large — it also keeps earning while you're away.

Upkeep ⛽

Every vehicle costs a little per second to run (more for planes). Idle vehicles still cost money, so keep them busy. Fuel cards lower this.

Hubs & network

Open new hubs in Network for more routes. You can also upgrade a hub (★) so its contracts pay more and appear more often — upgrade your busiest ones.

Levels

Delivering earns XP. Leveling unlocks new vehicles, hubs and upgrades, plus a cash bonus. XP is in the bar under the top menu — tap it for full stats.

The map

Pinch or scroll to zoom, drag to pan. Tap a moving vehicle to select it and have the camera follow it around Europe. Use ⊙ to reset the view.
